Square Praça do Amor - Av. São Sebastião

Praça do Amor is one of the busiest spots in Parnaíba. It is equipped with excellent lighting, offering a pleasant atmosphere and the sign “EU AMO PHB”, which has established itself as one of the main postcards of the city. All these characteristics make it one of the best places to practice physical activities. Furthermore, visitors can enjoy snacks from the food trucks installed in the square.

  • Duration: Walk - 1h10min; Race - 40min
  • Distance: 5.2 km
  • Peak Times: 4-8 pm
  • Reference points: Drogasil, Ideal Center, SmartFit, Parnaíba Shopping, Arena 1505 Beach, Mix Mateus, UBS São Sebastião

Lagoon Lagoa do Bebedouro

Lagoa do Bebedouro is one of the natural treasures that exist in Parnaíba. Lagoa do Bebedouro is an invitation to contemplation and leisure. Many visitors take advantage of its banks for moments of relaxation, family picnics, outdoor sports and even environmental education activities, raising awareness of the importance of preserving this rich ecosystem.

  • Duration: Walk - 45min; Race - 25min
  • Distance: 3.4 km
  • Peak times: 8-10 am; 4-8 pm
  • Landmarks: Bar da Lagoa, Restaurante da Lagoa, Beach Volleyball Court, Futsal Court, Recreational Area

Pier Quebra-Mar, Luís Correia

Quebra-Mar in Luís Correia is the ideal place to do physical activities and, at the same time, contemplate the beauty of the sea and get in touch with nature.

  • Duration: Walk - 53min; Race - 30min
  • Distance: 4 km
  • Peak times: 4-6 pm
  • Reference points: None
